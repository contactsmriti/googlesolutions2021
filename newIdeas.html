What we have in mind?


The main motive is to provide food and education to the poor and for that we have included sections like donation,volunteer in our website.

    People can volunteer as teacher to educate the poor people who cannot affort education..

    People can provide land or space where the teachers can teach.

    They can also help in delivering food from places or events where there was left over food after the function.

  • Donation

    People can donate whatever they wish to like food,money,clothes,books etc.
