Volunteer

Volunteer is to give yourself in service to a cause. It is about freely giving your time to help an organization, your community, or an individual.To volunteer means to act on behalf of something or someone else. It is done by people with a desire to do good. It is done willingly - by choice.

Why should we volunteer?

This question was asked from the people who was volunteering and their answers are as follows:

“To combat depression.”

“It gives my life purpose.”

“To put my skills to work for others.”

“So those I’m serving feel cared for.”

“To show my children and grandchildren the importance of giving back.”
